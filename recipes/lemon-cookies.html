Easy Lemon Cookies

Description

You see, I like lemon... like a lot. Give me a lemon meringue pie, or lemon squares, or lemon cookies and I am a happy camper! I've actually made cookies like this recipe from Allrecipes which Lissa named Easy Lemon Cookies. One because of how easy it is! Like just think of these as mini lemon cakes. Amazing with a good cup of coffee! And if you are like me, I enjoy making these in big batches as they tend to go quickly over the next while. Enjoy the lemon goodness!

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon cake package
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp of lemon extract, or do what I did: the zest and juice of 2 lemons... because I like lemon flavor! I like tart cookies!
  • 1/3 cup of icing sugar for on top: Only do a light sprinkle

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degree F
  2. Get a bowl, and put them ingredients in it and stir it like you just don't care! Drop about a teaspoon of dough into the icing sugar and roll in a ball.
  3. Get a pan, and place the rolled cookie balls about 2 inches apart.
  4. Put pan in the oven
  5. Now bake them till your soul tells you the texture is right! So about 6-10 minutes, but if you want crunchy, just do a bit more.
  6. Eat these with a good cup of coffee! That's how I do it!
